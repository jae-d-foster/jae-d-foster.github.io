Intro

Over the past three years at university, I have had the opportunity to design and create a range of projects across various TAS subject areas in different units. These include timber, metal, textiles, laser cutting, 3D printing, and coding/electromechanical systems. Each project has allowed me to develop practical skills, explore creative problem-solving, and apply design thinking principles across different technologies.
